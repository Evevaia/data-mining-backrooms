What is Data Mining?

Data mining is the process of collecting and analyzing your digital behavior. Every search, every click, every scroll, every pause. It gets recorded, sorted and sold. You are the product.

Hawthorne effect

The tendency to alter behavior when aware of being observed. The effect persists long after observation has ceased.

About the Project

PROJECT DON'T SCAN is a graphic design series that maps the silent architecture of data collection. Four posters. Four stages: consent, location, recording, profiling. Each one carries a QR code that demonstrates the very process it depicts. Created for Design & Visual Narrative at AKTO Art & Design College, under the supervision of Professor K. Theodorou, 2025–2026.
