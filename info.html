About the Project

PROJECT DON'T SCAN is a graphic design series curated by Vasilina Magklara, exploring the invisible stages of data mining, and the quiet surrender of information. It was created as part of the academic course Design & Visual Narrative under the supervision of Professor K. Theodorou at AKTO Art & Design College, 2025–2026.
